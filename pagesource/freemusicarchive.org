<h3><a href="http://freemusicarchive.org/label/MassDist/blog/Im_done_The_Complete_MASSDIST_On_The_FMA_Heres_One_Last_Random_Batch">The Complete MASS DIST Is Now Up On The FMA!!!! Here's One Last Random Batch...</a></h3>
<div class="imgbox">
<div class="stripey"><img src="https://freemusicarchive.org/file/images/entries/entry_image_file_-_entry_id-16016_-_20180223124615078.jpg?width=420&amp;height=280" alt="" align="top" width="420" height="280" /></div>
<div class="imgcaption"></div>
</div>
<div class="blog-txt blog-published published">
<div class="blog-body">
<p>Oh my god, I'm done!</p>
<p>Over 100 MASS DIST records made over 25 years are now up on the Free Music Archive! ALL OF THEM!</p>
<p>This took months and months, and years and years, and hours and hours every week. I'm relieved. I'm exhausted. I'm very proud.</p>
<p>I want to thank Jonah Rapino, Cesar Siguencia, and Cheyenne Hohman for all the help! And thanks to the Free Music Archive for being such a great home for all of our music!</p>
<p>To tie it up, one last batch of rando weirdo MASS DIST for you:</p>
<p><a href="http://freemusicarchive.org/music/Lance_Romance/Session_On_Devils_Day/">LANCE ROMANCE</a>, <a href="http://freemusicarchive.org/music/Dude_Tour/Best_Of/">DUDE TOUR</a>, <a href="http://freemusicarchive.org/music/Nature_Boy/Discography_1304/">NATURE BOY</a>, <a href="http://freemusicarchive.org/music/Don_Lennon/Live_At_The_Green_Street_Grill/">DON LENNON</a>, <a href="http://freemusicarchive.org/music/Godbois/Live_At_The_Green_Street_Grill_1548/">GODBOIS</a>, <a href="http://freemusicarchive.org/music/Tuttle_Music/Tuttle_Music/">TUTTLE MUSIC</a>, <a href="http://freemusicarchive.org/music/Grand_Island/Overblower/">GRAND ISLAND</a>, <a href="http://freemusicarchive.org/music/Open_Mic_Night/Open_Mic_Night/">OPEN MIC NIGHT</a>, <a href="http://freemusicarchive.org/music/WACSAC/Beatles_Split_1942/">WACSAC</a>, <a href="http://freemusicarchive.org/music/Maggotzoid/Maggotzoid/">MAGGOTZOID</a>, and <a href="http://freemusicarchive.org/music/Martyr/Mine_Folk_1/">MARTYR MINER</a></p>
<p>I got a couple ideas of things I'd like to upload to the FMA in the future, but the big pile is done. The mountain is levelled. I can rest. Move on to other ridiculous things.</p>
